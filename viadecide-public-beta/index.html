ViaDecide Public Beta Is Live

ViaDecide Team 15 July 2025 4 min read

After months of building in stealth, ViaDecide is now open to the public. India's first structured decision engine — built for students, founders, farmers, officials, and anyone in Bharat who needs to make a consequential decision and wants intelligence, not just information.

The core insight: India has a data abundance problem disguised as a data scarcity problem. The information exists — across Wikipedia, government portals, Wikinews, academic sources. What's missing is the synthesis layer that turns raw data into a decision.

What we built

ViaDecide v1 Beta ships with four core modules — each designed for a distinct part of the decision workflow:

Student Research Tool — Multi-source query engine that pulls from Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wikibooks, and Wikinews simultaneously, synthesizes the results, and produces a structured research brief. No hallucinations; every claim is source-attributed.

Decision Brief (PDF) — Takes a research session and exports a policy-grade brief: thesis statement, key takeaways, source contradictions, and recommended next steps. Sharable and downloadable.

Alchemist (Quiz Engine) — Converts any research brief into an assessment. Useful for educators, trainers, and anyone who wants to verify understanding rather than just consumption.

ONDC Demo — A live walkthrough of how Open Network for Digital Commerce flows work — built for sellers, operators, and policy evaluators exploring Bharat's open commerce stack.

Why now

The IndiaAI Mission announced a ₹10,371 crore investment in sovereign AI infrastructure. The National Data Governance Policy is taking shape. ONDC has crossed 10 million monthly transactions. The underlying rails are being laid — but the application layer for everyday decision-making remains largely empty.

ViaDecide is our answer to that gap. We're building the structured intelligence layer — not for Silicon Valley's problems, but for the specific, messy, multilingual, multi-context decisions that 1.4 billion Indians make every day.

What's next

The v1 Beta is a starting point. We're working on: multilingual input (Hindi, Marathi, Tamil first), offline-capable PWA support for low-connectivity regions, deeper ONDC integration, and an API for institutional partners who want to embed decision intelligence into their own systems.

If you're a student who needs better research tools, a founder evaluating a market, a farmer deciding on a crop, or an official drafting a brief — ViaDecide is built for you.

Try ViaDecide free during the public beta. No account required for the research tool.
